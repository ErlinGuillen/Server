📜 Rules of Senet

The Goal: Be the first to move all 5 pieces off the board (past Square 30).

  • Movement: Pieces follow an "S" path across three rows.
  • The Throw: You roll 4 sticks. The number of white sides facing up determines your move (1, 2, 3, 4, or 5).
  • Capturing: Landing on an opponent's piece swaps your positions, unless they are protected by a teammate.
  • Square 27 (Water): 𓈗 Falling here sends you back to the House of Rebirth (Square 15)!
